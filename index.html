Who We Are

Forming Leaders in the
Catholic Tradition

Founded by the Catholic Diocese of Aba under Most Rev. Joseph B. Whelan, C.S.Sp, Sacred Heart College has spent over six decades nurturing the minds, values, and character of young men across Eastern Nigeria. Returned to the Diocese in 2012, we continue a proud legacy.

  • 01

    Dedicated & Qualified Educators

    Experienced teachers and clergy committed to academic excellence and moral guidance in every classroom.

  • 02

    Comprehensive Nigerian Curriculum

    Sciences, arts, and commerce tracks with deep emphasis on Catholic values, discipline, and national standards.

  • 03

    A Brotherhood & Community

    A disciplined student body united in academic pursuit, sporting tradition, and Christian brotherhood.

Programmes & Formation

Key Areas of
Excellence

At SAHARCO, formation goes beyond the classroom. We develop the whole person — intellectually, spiritually, physically, and socially — in the finest tradition of Catholic education.

Academic Excellence

Strong WAEC & NECO examination results with consistent distinction-level performances across subjects.

Sports & Athletics

A proud inter-house and inter-school sporting tradition fostering discipline, teamwork, and physical wellness.

Moral & Spiritual Growth

Catholic values, daily prayer, retreat programs, and sacramental life forming men of deep faith and integrity.

Boarding Life

A structured, safe, and conducive boarding environment where brotherhood and personal responsibility are built.

Science & Technology

Well-equipped laboratories and learning facilities supporting STEM education and practical skill development.

Leadership Formation

Prefect systems, student councils, clubs, and debate societies cultivating tomorrow's civic and professional leaders.
