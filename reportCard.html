A Legacy of Faith,
Discipline & Excellence

Sacred Heart College Eziukwu Aba (SAHARCO) is a prestigious Catholic boys' secondary school founded in 1957 by the Catholic Diocese of Aba. Nestled in the heart of Aba, Abia State, we form young men of character, competence, and compassion — equipped to lead in every sphere of life.

Our mission is rooted in the Sacred Heart of Jesus: to bear witness to truth through academic rigor, spiritual formation, and service to humanity.

Our Mission

Rooted in the
Sacred Heart

"Testimonium Perhibere Veritati" — To Bear Witness to the Truth

Our motto captures the spirit of SAHARCO. Every dimension of school life — from classroom instruction to chapel worship, from sports fields to community service — is oriented toward forming young men who will bear witness to truth and integrity in the world.

Founded under the Catholic Diocese of Aba, Sacred Heart College holds fast to the educational philosophy of the Church: that the development of faith, reason, and character are inseparable. We are not merely an academic institution; we are a community of formation.

How to Apply

Admissions into JS1 and SS1 are conducted annually. Follow the steps below to begin your son's SAHARCO journey.

1
Obtain and complete the admission form from the school's administrative office or online portal.
2
Submit the form with copies of birth certificate, Primary 6 leaving certificate (for JS1), and BECE results (for SS1).
3
Sit the entrance examination conducted by the school on a designated date.
4
Await results and, upon offer of admission, complete the acceptance and payment of school fees.
